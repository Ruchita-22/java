    Regular Expression:
    ===================

    Regular is a pattern used to match a character of combination
    in string

   Regular expresssion is used for search a string based on 
   particular pattern

    expressions or pattern
    ------------------------

    [0-9] -- it will accept one character from 0 to 9 digits

    [a-z] -- it will accept one character from a to z

    [A-Z] -- it will accept one character from A to Z

    [.]  --- all character 


  quantifiers:
  ------------

   *  -- indicates zero or more times the character occurence.

  +    -- atleast one time occur and more times

   ? --- zero or exactly one time occurance

  ^  --  negatation  operation


  ex:
  ---

  [abc]  --- a or b or c

  /a+/  --- a or aaa

  /ab* / ---  b or bbbb

  /ab? / --- 0 or b


 /[^abc] --- it will accept d to z

  {n}  --no of occurrence
   {n,m} --- m -- maxmimum  of occurrence from n

      {9,5} -- 5  of 9 
  
  [0-9]{10,5}
